About Simply FurEver

Simply FurEver is a non-profit organization that promotes the rehoming of pre-trained dogs and cats. It has been statistically proven that 96% of animals that are returned to shelters or rescues are brought back due to simple training issues such as house breaking, barking, jumping, and chewing. Simply FurEver has teamed up with local rescues and shelters across America to provide better pet ownership education, training tools, and access to rescues and/or shelters who already have well-trained pets waiting for their FurEver homes.

How do I become a foster parent to a Simply FurEver pet-in-training?

Foster homes are always the best alternative to a shelter or rescue facility! Fostering gives animals the chance to live in an actual home where they will encounter real day to day life in a home environment. Becoming a foster parent to a Simply FurEver pet-in-training does require either a background in pet training or completion of a Simply FurEver Pet Trainer Program. To find out more, please submit a request for a Simply FurEver Foster Parent Application in the contact form at the very bottom of this page.

What pet care items are needed most?

The basics are always needed!
  • Pet food (both wet and dry, please!)
  • Treats (especially TRAINING treats!)
  • Leashes and Collars
  • Toys (both dog and cat)
  • Grooming supplies (brushes, shampoos, flea treatments, etc.)
  • Bowls, Crates and Beds (all sizes)
